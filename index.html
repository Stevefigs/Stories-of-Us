Stories of Us

these works come together to illustrate the way art depicts stories, Those of different eras and places. These pieces are stories of people. Some depict people in daily life, others depict transcendence and the immortal world of Gods. Stories of Us show the human tendency of immortalizing individuals through art, and how history plays its role in that.
